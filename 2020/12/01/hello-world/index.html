$\mathbf{Theorem \ 7.1. \ (Cauchy’s \ residue \ theorem).}$

Let G be a simply connected domain and let P $= \{a_1, . . . , a_n\} \subseteq$ G. Let $\gamma$ be a simple closed path in G surrounding $a_1, . . . , a_n$ with positive orientation. For f $\subseteq \mathcal{H}(G \ P)$ we have
$$\int_{\gamma}{f\left( z \right) dz=2}\pi i\sum_{j=1}^n{\text{Res}\left( f,a_j \right)}$$
